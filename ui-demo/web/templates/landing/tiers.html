DaaS Tiered Packages

Choose your engagement

CDPI's Digital Public Infrastructure as a Packaged Solution (DaaS) is offered in three tiers — Spark, Boost, and Scale — representing increasing levels of deployment scope, programmatic depth, and institutional embedding. Each tier maps to the same DaaS v2.0 requirement IDs, so countries can move between tiers without re-architecting.

Tier 1 · Spark
Spark
Minimum viable deployment. The lightest path to demonstrating that verifiable credentials work in your country.
Active rollout: 16 weeks
Post-rollout support: 10 weeks
Cloud hosting: 8 months
Use cases: 2 end-to-end
Best for: Countries that need to prove feasibility.
  • ✓ Foundational VC infrastructure (issuance, store, verification)
  • ✓ 2 issuers + corresponding verifiers, end-to-end
  • ✓ SSO integration
  • ✓ L1 / L2 support
  • ✓ Cloud hosting, basic monitoring
  • ✓ AI-assisted development tooling
  • − No outreach playbook
  • − No ecosystem expansion
  • − No sustainability planning
Tier 2 · Boost
Boost
The closest equivalent to the original DaaS v2.0 scope. Full programmatic engagement with outreach, ecosystem onboarding, and national policy support, on a 24-week active timeline.
Active rollout: 24 weeks
Post-rollout support: 24 weeks
Cloud hosting: 12 months
Use cases: 2–5 use cases
Best for: Countries with momentum and clear use cases ready for ecosystem deployment.
  • ✓ Everything in Spark
  • ✓ 2–5 end-to-end use cases
  • ✓ Outreach playbook (issuer & acceptor adoption roadmap)
  • ✓ Ecosystem onboarding (additional issuers, verifiers)
  • ✓ National credentialing policy support
  • ✓ L1 / L2 support, monitoring
  • ✓ 12-month cloud hosting
  • ✓ Intelligent user-facing AI services
Tier 3 · Scale
Scale
Advanced capabilities and deep institutional embedding. The full strategic deliverables suite, denser parallel workstreams, L1–L3 support, designed for all-you-can-imagine rollouts with multilateral funding alignment.
Active rollout: 24 weeks (parallel workstreams)
Post-rollout support: 24 weeks
Cloud hosting: 12 months
Use cases: 5+ use cases
Best for: Countries committing to unmatched-scale deployment with strong DPI Owner capacity.
  • ✓ Everything in Boost
  • ✓ 5+ use cases with parallel workstreams
  • ✓ Operational & business plan
  • ✓ Multilateral funding alignment
  • ✓ Impact whitepaper
  • ✓ Ecosystem mapping
  • ✓ L1 / L2 / L3 support (extended 12 months)
  • ✓ 12-month cloud hosting
  • ✓ Advanced AI-driven ecosystem intelligence
A note on tier selection

Tier selection follows the requirements you assemble in your country scope document — not the other way around. The tier label is the consequence of which functional requirements your country selects from the DaaS v2.0 matrix. If your requirements span tiers, choose the higher tier. Countries that succeed with Spark have a natural upgrade path to Boost and then Scale, with full requirement traceability between tiers.
