Verifier Request

Paste the OID4VP link from the verifier, or scan their QR code.

Show your credential as a QR code. The QR contains the full credential payload (PixelPass-encoded: base45 + zlib). A verifier scans it with Inji Verify or any compatible verifier app and runs cryptographic verification locally — no server round-trip needed. This works offline.
For verifier-initiated OID4VP flows, use the Respond to Verifier tab — that's where the verifier sends you a request URL and your wallet responds.
